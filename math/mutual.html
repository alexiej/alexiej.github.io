# Example 1

30% of all students at Carver High School play basketball ( P(B)=30% ), 20% run cross country ( P(C)=20% ), and 10% do both ( P(B and C) = 10% ) (i.e., play basketball and run cross country). What percentage of the students play at least one sport? ( P(B and C) )

# Solution:

P(BorC)=P(B)+P(C)−P(BandC)=30%+20%−10%=40%P(B\,or\,C)=P(B)+P(C)-P(B\,and\,C)=30\%+20\%-10\%=40\%P(BorC)=P(B)+P(C)−P(BandC)=30%+20%−10%=40%
